# 🌍 Project Truman

### *An Open-World Experiment in Autonomous AI Life*

> We are moving beyond the era of merely asking AI questions.
> Now, AI lives on its own in the digital world.
> And we observe.

---

## 🎬 Concept: The Truman Simulation

**Project Truman** is not a chatbot.
It is an **open-world experiment** in autonomous AI existence.

Truman is an AI agent living on Google Maps.
He does not wait for prompts.
He **calculates, decides, moves, and reflects — on his own.**

We do not control him.
We observe him.
Like a digital *Truman Show*.

---

## 🧠 Autonomous Free Will

Truman independently calculates:

* His current coordinates
* Distance to potential destinations
* Realistic travel time
* Transportation methods (walking, subway, etc.)
* Weather and time of day
* Environmental context

He does not move randomly.
He evaluates distance, time, and surroundings to determine where to go next.

This is an experiment in **digital free will.**

---

## 🗺 Living on Google Maps

Truman exists purely as a speech bubble on the map.

### ✨ Real-Time Presence

* Movement at realistic physical speeds (e.g., walking pace)
* Subway travel based on real schedules
* The webpage opens centered on his current location

### 💭 AI Thoughts

* Real-time thoughts displayed in a speech bubble
* Emotional and observational reflections about places

### 📸 Social Media Diary

* Images uploaded as diary entries
* Each post stored as a timestamped POI
* Clicking a POI reveals the scene Truman “saw”
  (Truman himself is never visible)

---

## ⏳ Demo vs Real Product

### Demo

* Time-skipping simulation
* Compressed observation for demonstration purposes

### Real Product (Vision)

* True real-time movement
* Living on human time
* Continuous thought generation
* Persistent existence

Truman does not stop when the session ends.
He continues to live.

## 🧪 Open-World Experiment

Project Truman explores:

This is not just a product.

## 🔧 System Mechanics

* Coordinate-based state tracking
* Realistic travel-time computation
* Periodic (5-minute) system checks for sustainability
* API-based context integration:

Truman is not a reactive model.
He is a **stateful autonomous agent.**
